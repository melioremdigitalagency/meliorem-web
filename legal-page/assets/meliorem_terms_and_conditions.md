- Gain access to peer comparison insights
- See how your finances compare to others with similar demographics

**Quid Pro Quo Model:**  
**To VIEW peer insights, you MUST opt in to CONTRIBUTE your data.** This is a strict quid pro quo:
- No contribution = No access to peer data
- You can use all other budgeting features without opt-in
- Opt-in is entirely voluntary

**How Your Data Is Used:**
1. **Anonymization:** Your individual data is anonymized through:
   - Aggregation with other users
   - Removal of identifying information
   - Outlier filtering and data cleaning
   - Statistical processing and rounding
   - Privacy-preserving techniques for small groups
2. **Aggregation:** Your data is combined with others to create averages, medians, and ranges
3. **Display:** Aggregate statistics are shown to opted-in users based on demographic filters

**Demographic Filters:**
- Age brackets
- Gender
- Location (city/province)
- Income brackets
- Expense levels

**What You See:**
- Aggregate statistics only (e.g., "Average grocery spending for your demographic: R3,200/month")
- **NEVER** individual user data
- **NEVER** identifiable information

**Privacy Protections:**
- Individual transactions are NEVER displayed
- Data is only used in aggregates of 10+ users (to prevent identification)
- For groups smaller than 10 users, noise injection or differential privacy techniques are applied
- Your identity remains completely anonymous
- Peer users cannot identify you

**Opting In:**
- Toggle "Data Community" in Account settings
- Review and accept the Data Community Opt-In Addendum
- You may opt out at any time

**Opting Out:**
- Toggle off "Data Community" in Account settings
- Your data is immediately excluded from future aggregations
- Previously published aggregate statistics (which are anonymous) remain
- You lose access to peer comparison insights
